CRYSTAL COVE ISLAND

Crystal Cove Island, located off the coast of Boracay in the Philippines, is a popular island retreat known for its scenic beauty, crystal-clear waters, and lush landscapes. The island is approximately 2.6 kilometers from the main Boracay island and is accessible via boat rides that typically depart from the mainland.

ABOUT THE PLACE

Trekking

A fun-filled trek for the whole family! Hike to two stunning coves—it's easy, breezy, and the views are totally worth the steps!

Snorkling

Snorkel in crystal-clear coves—if the sea's calm, dive in! Cove 1 or Cove 2, every splash is a blast!

Fish Feeding

Snorkeling and fishing? Absolutely! Grab some bread or biscuits—drop them in and watch the fish swarm in seconds!

TRAVEL GUIDE

1 - Cabanatuan City

Start your adventure in Cabanatuan City, where you can catch a bus or a private van to Clark International Airport (CRK). Buses take about 2 to 3 hours and cost between ₱300 to ₱500, while a faster private van can get you there in 1.5 to 2.5 hours for ₱400 to ₱600.

2 - Clark International Airport

From Clark, hop on a quick flight to Caticlan Airport (MPH)—Boracay’s closest airport. The flight takes approximately 1 hour, with prices ranging from ₱1,500 to over ₱5,000, depending on how early you book.

3 - Caticlan

Once you land in Caticlan, take a tricycle to the jetty port, which costs around ₱100 to ₱150 (be sure to negotiate!). From the jetty, ride a ferry for about 15 to 20 minutes to Boracay Island. The ferry fare is ₱30, and combined with the terminal and environmental fees (₱300), your total comes to ₱330.

4 - Boracay

Now on Boracay, it's time to reach Crystal Cove Island. You can join an island hopping tour for ₱800 to ₱1,500+ per person, or charter a private boat for around ₱1,500 to ₱3,500+, depending on the boat size and your haggling skills. Either way, the boat ride takes about 15 to 20 minutes across gorgeous turquoise waters.

5 - Crystal Cove Island

Altogether, the estimated one-way cost per person is about ₱3,130 if you go with the budget route (van, budget flight, shared tour), or up to ₱7,580 if you opt for a more comfortable experience (van, flexible flight, private boat).
